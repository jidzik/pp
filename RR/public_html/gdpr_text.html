GDPR Advice, Consultancy, Training and Design


Although EU GDPR adopts “risk-based compliance” and encourages formal risk analysis, it does not mention how organizations should evaluate, assess and measure the risk. Suggested concept of “privacy by design” by GDPR should be applicable to all new business initiatives and technology developments, but how to evaluate existing processes and environments? Since all operations must comply with GDPR regulations, how to assess and manage “legacy” designs and systems, till they reach their end-of-life?

General Data Protection Regulation EU 2016/679 describes and regulates how and why personal data should be processed and used by business entities: “… the protection of natural persons regarding the processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data …”. GDPR regulations are applicable worldwide, not only to businesses in EU “… applies to the processing of personal data of data subjects who are in the Union …” or monitor their behaviour – art. 3 of GDPR
